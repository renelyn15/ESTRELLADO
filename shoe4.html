Puma

The Puma Speedcat OG Red White is a bold performance shoe made for speed whose design was inspired by the racetrack.

The Red White Puma Speedcat OG has a lightweight paneled leather upper in Puma's bold For All Time Red color. A red leather overlay forms the toe cap, which also bears a white Puma logo on the outer side. White suede Puma Formstrip racing stripes appear on each side of the shoe above the red heel panel, while the Puma brand name and logo appear in metallic-gold letters on the outer side right above it. The tongue and heel also feature metallic-gold Puma branding. The rubber outsole is black and patterned for traction.
