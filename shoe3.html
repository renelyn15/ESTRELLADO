The adidas Originals Women's Superstar Sneaker has received mixed reviews from customers. On the other hand, some customers found the sneakers to be true-to-size, but had personal preferences regarding the length and stiffness of the tongue. They mentioned that the shoes were nice looking and comfortable overall. Overall, the adidas Originals Women's Superstar Sneaker has received mixed reception. While some customers have had positive experiences with the product, others have complained about issues with sizing and quality.
